Tell us your story! What products do you sell thanks to PrestaShop?

Created in 2010, Bobbies is a Parisian house of exceptional shoes and leather goods. Designed in Paris, our creations are made in Portugal from raw and noble leather of Italian origin. We imagine with passion singular but easy to wear pieces, timeless but in the air of time, cheeky but iconic. Our vocation: to wrap women’s hearts, inspire men’s hearts and see them beating together on the Parisian pavement!

What value does PrestaShop add to your store?

We chose PrestaShop for many reasons, starting with the stability of the platform and its many PHP resources available for the development of our site. PrestaShop allowed us to create a multilingual and multi-currency e-commerce site at a very reasonable total cost.
